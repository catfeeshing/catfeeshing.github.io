Plans

 

      Based on your amount of views, you will receive a recommendation for one of the four following plans. Evidently, the final decision for what plan to use is up to you.   

   

          We offer two payment choices for each plan: either pay month-by-month if you don't intend to stay for the duration of the plan or pay the cost for the full stay all at once. Additionally, depending on how hard your video makes us laugh, we may offer you a free stay.   

   

      Once your plan expires, to apply for another stay, you must submit another application. While the newly submitted application is waiting to be reviewed, you may not stay in the facility. Should you stay, you will be asked to leave. Individuals who refuse to cooperate will be escorted out of the building by security.   

   

      The view requirement for each plan is only one factor that determines the duration of your stay. The number of views is simply a general guideline to give you a good idea of how long your stay might be. Do not take them seriously and instead refer to them only when needed. The final decision is up to the staff that reviews videos.
