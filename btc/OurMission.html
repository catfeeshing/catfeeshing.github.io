Brackwood Train Co. was founded in 1896 by Alaena Jitpapaya.

We are dedicated to bringing you the best train experience possible while keeping our prices affordable.

Trains aren't what they used to be. Just a century or two ago, they were all the rage. Everyone could get to everywhere on a train and train tracks were being installed like crazy. It was the brink of a new era! No longer would people have to depend on unreliable forms of traveling. But now we are ushering in a new era, one that is centered around cars and both economy and environment-friendly ways of transportation. Trains aren't the most efficient now and they emit lots of carbon dioxide. Here at Brackwood Train Co., we're trying to change that.

Our newest train model, the Brackwood Sungazer, is a train that runs solely on solar power. It has all the components of a regular train and can perform just as well, if not better. The train's design was based on that of a bullet train- very sleek, modern, and, most importantly, aerodynamic.

For a cheaper alternative to the Sungazer, we offer the Brackwood Duo. The Duo is a hybrid train designed to be very aerodynamic, though it's incomparable to the Sungazer.
